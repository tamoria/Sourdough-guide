How to Feed Your Starter Video

When feeding your starter, you may use any flour you want. Currently, your starter feeds on organic whole wheat flour. If you ever want to change it, you may do so gradually. Switch out the flour little by little during 4 to 5 days.

When using your starter, you want to make sure it is well-fed and healthy. Feed your starter 10 to 12 hours before use and let it sit out on the kitchen counter in room temperature.
